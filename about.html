Our Story

Building the Future of Personal Finance

We believe everyone deserves a financial advisor that truly understands them. One that never sleeps, never judges, and evolves with every moment of your life.

Our Mission

To democratize financial intelligence by creating an AI that adapts to each individual's unique circumstances, psychology, and goals — making world-class financial guidance accessible to everyone.

Our Vision

A world where financial stress is eliminated. Where everyone, regardless of income volatility or complexity, has a tireless ally helping them build the life they dream of.

The Problem

Finance Wasn't Built for Real People

78% of people live paycheck to paycheck
59M gig workers with unpredictable income
$1.4T lost annually to emotional spending
0.1% can afford a personal financial advisor

Traditional financial tools were designed for a world of stable jobs, predictable paychecks, and rational decision-making. That world no longer exists for most people.

The gig economy is exploding. Income is volatile. Spending is emotional. And the financial industry's response? More spreadsheets. More generic advice. More shame.

We're building something radically different.

Ethical AI

Our Commitment to Responsible AI

With great power comes great responsibility. We've built AURA with robust ethical guardrails that protect you at every step.

  • No Predatory Recommendations: We never suggest products that harm your financial health
  • Bias Auditing: Regular third-party audits ensure our AI treats all users fairly
  • Explainable Decisions: You can always ask AURA why it made a recommendation
  • Human Override: You're always in control. AURA suggests, you decide
  • Mental Health Awareness: We detect financial distress and connect users to resources
